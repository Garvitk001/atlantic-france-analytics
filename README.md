# 🇫🇷 France Top 50 Music Intelligence Dashboard
**Atlantic Recording Corporation | Market Strategy & Audience Analytics**

An interactive Business Intelligence and Data Science dashboard designed to analyze audience preferences, content compliance, and structural track characteristics in the French music market.

## 📋 Executive Context
The French music market has distinct characteristics, including a strong emphasis on cultural sensitivity, greater scrutiny of explicit content, and a historical preference for structured album formats over rapid-release singles. 

For **Atlantic Recording Corporation**, understanding these specific market dynamics is essential for:
* Making data-driven release format decisions.
* Managing explicit-content risk.
* Formulating playlist pitching strategies.
* Successfully localizing global music releases.

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Despite access to France Top 50 playlist data, there is a need to clarify:
1. How explicit content performs relative to clean tracks.
2. Whether French charts currently prefer singles or album tracks.
3. How song duration aligns with listener acceptance.
4. Whether larger albums dilute or strengthen individual track performance.

This project solves this by delivering content-centric intelligence tailored to the French music market, empowering Atlantic Records to design culturally aligned music strategies grounded in data.

## 🏗️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend / UI:** Streamlit
* **Data Manipulation:** Pandas
* **Data Visualization:** Plotly (Express & Graph Objects)
* **Language:** Python 3.8+

## 📊 Core Analytical Modules
The dashboard is divided into 7 interactive tabs:
1. **Market Overview:** Trending artists and songs by popularity.
2. **Content Compliance Analysis:** Explicit vs. clean content distribution and popularity mapping.
3. **Release Format Preference:** Single vs. album track representation and performance.
4. **Song Structure Insights:** Track duration histograms and rank-alignment (Short, Medium, Long).
5. **Album Structure Impact:** Scatter plots and correlation analysis to identify album dilution effects.
6. **Rank Tier Analysis:** Attribute density across Top 10, Top 25, and Top 50 segments.
7. **Audience Preference Insights:** Executive summary panel of "preferred content profiles."
